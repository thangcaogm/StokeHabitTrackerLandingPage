FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Stoke free to use? +

Yes. You can track up to 3 active habits free, forever — no account required. Premium unlocks unlimited habits, advanced stats, widgets, the Apple Watch app, and custom themes.

Does my data sync across devices? +

Stoke is offline-first and stores everything on your device. You can optionally enable iCloud sync to keep your habits in step across iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. Your data is never sent to our servers.

How does the free trial work? +

Monthly and Annual plans include a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime from your Apple ID settings before then at no cost.

What happens if I miss a day? +

Daily habits have a grace period until end of day, and scheduled rest days never break your streak. Stoke's streak engine is timezone- and DST-aware, so traveling won't unfairly cool your flame.

Which devices are supported? +

iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch running iOS / iPadOS / watchOS 26 or later, with Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets.
